Chicken Salad Sandwich

Description:

This is a Chicken Salad Sandwich by Javon's Kitchen. This is a very tasty recipe that can be used with any leftover chicken

Ingredients:

  • Mayonnaise
  • Chicken
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Black Pepper
  • Purple Onions
  • Mustard (optional)
  • Sandwich Buns

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Shred chicken
  2. Step 2: Mix shredded chicken with mayonnaise
  3. Step 3: Season with black pepper
  4. Step 4: Dice onions and add to chicken salad
  5. Step 5: Line buns with tomato & cucumber
  6. Step 6: Paste salad mix onto tomatoes & cucumber, add mustard if needed
