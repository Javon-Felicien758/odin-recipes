Cocoa Tea (Caribbean)

Description:

Classic hot beverage, made of cocoa from the caribbean

Ingredients:

  • Cocoa Stick or Cocoa Balls
  • Condensed Milk
  • Custard Powder / Cornstatch or Flour
  • Cinnamon (Sticks or Powder)
  • Vanilla Extract or Vanilla Essense
  • Dried Bay Leaf
  • Nutmeg
  • Star Anaise
  • Sugar (optional)

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Grate Cocoa Stick / Cocoa Ball into a container
  2. Step 2: In a cup mix cornstach, custard or flour with warm water to make a thickner solution
  3. Step 2: In a pan add Bay Leaf, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Star Anaise and boil for 5 minutes
  4. Step 3: Add grated cocoa into the pan, boil on medium heat for an additional 15 minutes or until cocoa is dissolved
  5. Step 1: Add condensed milk until desired sweetness
  6. Step 4: Add thickner solution until desired thickness is achieved
  7. Step 5: Remove cinnamon (stick), bayleaf & star anaise from the pan
  8. Step 6: Boil for an additional 2-3 minutes, add sugar if desired sweetness is not met
