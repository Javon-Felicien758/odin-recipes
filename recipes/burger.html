Burger

Description:

This is a Tasty Burger by Javon's Kitchen. This is a twist on the traditional bacon cheese burger giving it more zest and flavor than ever!

Ingredients:

  • Bacon
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Beef Patty 1/4 lb
  • Badia Seasoning
  • Purple Onions
  • Spicy Thousand Island Sauce
  • Sesame Seed Burger Buns

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Toast buns until crisp
  2. Step 2: Apply Spicy Thousand Island Sauce to buns
  3. Step 3: Wash & place vegetables on buns
  4. Step 4: Season beef patty with Badia Seasoning, grill patty & bacon
  5. Step 5: While still on the grill, put cheese on top of patty for a short while, then remove
  6. Step 6: Pur grill cheese patty on bun, along with bacon, tomato and purple onions
